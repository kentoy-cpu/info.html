About the Mayor

Thadeo Jonkie Ouano

Dedicated Leader of Mandaue City

Education: BA Political Science, MBA Public Administration

In Service Since: June 30, 2025

Thadeo "Jonkie" Jovito Mabanag Ouano, Jr., born on November 16, 1979, in Mandaue City, Cebu, is a Filipino politician currently serving as a Board Member representing the 6th District of Cebu Province. He is the son of the late Thadeo "Teddy" Ouano, a former mayor of Mandaue City, and the brother of Emmarie "Lolypop" Ouano-Dizon, the current representative of Cebu's 6th District in the House of Representatives.
